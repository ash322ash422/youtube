## Video Length Strategy

* **Short Conceptual Videos:** 5–10 min (introductions, key concepts)
* **Medium Walkthroughs:** 12–15 min (probability, confidence intervals, CLT)
* **Longer Applications:** 15–20 min (regression, hypothesis testing, evaluation metrics)

Aim for **40–50 videos total**, each digestible but complete. Learners prefer shorter focused videos over very long ones.
